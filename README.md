## üìñ Overview
The **Garage Management System (GMS)** is a Salesforce-based cloud application developed to digitalize and automate operations in automotive service centers.  
It provides modules for **customer management, service scheduling, mechanic assignment, billing, inventory tracking, and performance reporting** ‚Äî all integrated within the Salesforce Lightning platform.

This system helps garage owners and service providers streamline daily operations, eliminate manual errors, and improve customer satisfaction.

---

## üéØ Objectives
- Automate day-to-day garage operations through Salesforce.  
- Maintain detailed and centralized records of customers, vehicles, and services.  
- Enable quick appointment booking and mechanic allocation.  
- Generate bills automatically and track payment history.  
- Create dashboards for visual analytics on business performance.  
- Improve overall service efficiency and customer engagement.  

---

## üß© Problem Statement
Garages and auto-service centers often manage records manually, leading to inefficiency and poor customer experience. There is no centralized system to manage customer details, appointments, or billing.  
This project aims to create a **Salesforce-based Garage Management System** to overcome these challenges by providing a **fully automated, scalable, and user-friendly platform**.

---

## üí° Proposed Solution
The proposed system automates all garage-related activities by integrating them into a single Salesforce application.  
- Customers can register their vehicles and book services.  
- Admins can assign mechanics and monitor service progress.  
- Billing and reports are generated automatically.  
- Dashboards offer insights into performance, costs, and workload.  

This ensures transparency, accuracy, and operational efficiency.

---

## ‚öôÔ∏è Key Features
- **Customer Management:** Register and manage customer and vehicle details.  
- **Service Requests:** Track job details, parts used, and service history.  
- **Mechanic Assignment:** Allocate tasks automatically based on availability.  
- **Billing & Invoicing:** Generate bills automatically after service completion.  
- **Inventory Management:** Track spare parts stock and reorder levels.  
- **Reports & Dashboards:** Visual representation of revenue, efficiency, and workload.

## üß© Installation and Setup
1. Sign up for a **Salesforce Developer Org** ‚Üí [developer.salesforce.com](https://developer.salesforce.com/).  
2. Create the following **Custom Objects**:  
   - `Customer__c`, `Vehicle__c`, `Service_Record__c`, `Billing__c`, `Mechanic__c`  
3. Define **Relationships** between objects (lookup/master-detail).  
4. Add **Automation Rules** (Process Builder, Workflow Rules).  
5. Design **Lightning Pages** and **Tabs** for navigation.  
6. Build **Reports and Dashboards** for analysis.  
7. Test all modules using sample data.
